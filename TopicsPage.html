A. Mental Health

Mental health refers to the state of a well-being. It allows everyone to successfully deal with life's challenges and discover their capabilities.
It is an important aspect of our health. Mental health is an essential human privilage. Mental health talks about our feelings, emotions, thinking, and our moods.
Just like our physical health, we also have to take care of our mental health because it involves our feelings and our way of thinking.

B. Emotional Health

Emotional health refers to how we feel and think. It is about our ability to cope with our negative and positive emotions.
Emotional health doesn't always mean that we are happy. Being an emotionally healthy person can give you the ability to control your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.
Being emotionally healthy also gives you the ability to cope up witth life's challenges.

C. Physical Health

Physical health is about how well our body and organs work efficiently. Every person has it's own physical health; some are healthy, some are not.
Physical health plays a very important role in our overall health. Caring about our physical health can also help your mental health. So, all healths are connected to one another.
Physical health can simply be defined as the normal functions of our organs and body.
