Physical Health

Physical health is closely linked to our mental health. A good mental health, also means a good physical health. It is an important factor in creating a healthy lifestyle. Physical health defines the normal functions of our body. It is the one who represents a human well-being. It involves how our body grows, how it moves, how it feels, and how we care for it.

Maintaining a good physical health can help lower the risks of you getting infected by diseases, like cancer and heart-related diseases. Another benefit of having a good physical healh is that it you'll have lower chances of getting injured and fatigue.

Healthy Lifestyle

Healthy lifestyles are important as they are the ones who helps you in avoiding diseases. Having an unhealthy lifestyle means that you are more in danger of getting affected by different diseases.

Heathy lifestyles are not only used in avoiding diseases, it can also help you in coping up with life problems, and also in improving the quality of your life. If you want to stay fit and healthy until you're old, try making a healthy lifestyle for yourself. Sometimes, when you get advice from people who has their own healthy lifestyle, it may be complicated and difficult to understand. But living a healthy lifestyle doesn't have to be complicated.

Factors of a Healhty Lifestyle

If you want to start living with a healthy lifestyle, here are 5 factors that you should begin with:

  • Have a healthy balanced diet.
  • Have plenty of sleep.
  • Drink lots of water.
  • Regular exercises.
  • Always keep on cheking your mental and physical health.

Importance of Living Healthy

Staying healthy is an important part in our daily life. We can achieve it in many ways like eating healthy and drinking water. If you still aren't convinced, these are 8 reasons why staying healthy is important:

1. Extends your Lifespan.
This is one of the main reasons why people shift their lives in living healthy. A study shows that if you drink alcohol in a moderate amount, avoid smoking, do regular exercises, and having a healthy diet can extend your lifespan by 14 years.
2. Cheaper Life Insurance.
If your body is healthy and your mind is healthy, you wouldn't have a problem paying a lot for your life insurace. If ever you are in your death bed, you'll only pay less since you're pretty much old at that time by now.
3. Cheaper Medical Costs.
You may be healthy, but you can also be infected by minor diseases such as cough and colds. Because they are only minor diseases, you'll only have to pay less in medical bills since it isn't really life threatening.
4. Become a Role Model to Others.
When you do exercises regularly and always eat healthy foods, you might inspire the people around you. They will aspire to become like you.
5. Lower Stress.
In our modern society, where even the young and old experiences stress, they become unhealthy. While people who lives in a healthy lifestyle doesn't really experience stress frequently and they have lower levels of anxiety.
6. Appreciate Yourself.
Living healthy allows you to appreciate all of the things you did for yourself. You'll feel more confident as you grow older because you feel a lot better than the past.
7. Increases Fertility.
If you are planning to have a lot of kids in the future, it is best to stay healthy and fit. Harvard studies shows that males who exercises and lives healthy have a higher sperm level.
